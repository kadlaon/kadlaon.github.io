 Educational Background

I took up Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at the University of Southeastern Philippines. I was one of the project leaders of the Integrated UNIISYS project. I led the project eTudlo a web-based subsystem of UNIISYS specifically designed for students who would want to offer tutorials to anyone that is willing to learn on particular subjects that he/she finds difficult. These students are ought to be merely from University of Southeastern Philippines (USeP). eTudlo is mainly an online posting of subjects and schedules from service providers.

Months after I finished my tertiary education, I then took Master in Information Technology at the University of the Immaculate Conception. It was during my enrollment in this post-graduate study that I was exposed to the Philippine Computing Science Congress and that motivated me to jump from the Information Technology field to Computer Science.

Privileged enough, 2 years after I earned my masters degree, I was able to get a St. La Salle Scholarship to fund my Ph.D. I then took a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the De La Salle University and I am currently in my dissertation writing stage.
